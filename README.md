## ü§ù How to Contribute

We welcome contributions from anyone! Here‚Äôs a quick guide:

1. **Fork this repository** to create your copy.  
2. **Add your eBook** in the correct folder (`Genre/Year`).  
3. **Name your file** properly: `Author - Book_Title.pdf`.  
4. **Commit your changes** with a clear message: `Add [Book Title] by [Author]`

5. **Open a Pull Request** to the main repository.  
6. Your contribution will be reviewed and merged.
